Dear Friend and Believer in Jesus Christ

You have reached this page because somebody believes that you would want to join a coordinated effort to pray for Namibia.

One of our friends received an instruction from the Lord Jesus to bring together a group of at least 1000 people to pray for our country Namibia. He discussed this with a group of trusted friends and they agreed that this was indeed an instruction from the Lord. This team is now working to assemble a network of at least 1000 praying individuals. We are however hoping that many more people will eventually join this network.

The Lord also instructed us that this network should consists of groups of twelve. Every prayer (praying individual) will be a member of a group of twelve, but each member will also aim to invite eleven more people into the prayer network, making another group of twelve.

This is what you are signing up for.

  1. To pray for Namibia as the Holy Spirit leads you.
  2. To pray according to the prayer requests that the network will send to you from time to time.
  3. To recruit more people to pray for the country by first speaking to them and then using the "Send an invitation" function of the website to finalise the recruitment.
  4. Stay connected with the people in your immediate network, through messaging, getting together for coffee and telephone conversations with the aim to motivate one another to pray.

Signing up to join this network is completely voluntary. Please do not join if you feel pressured. Only join if you truly feel in your heart that you should be part of this network.
