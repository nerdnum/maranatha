The Calling

Namibia is a country in spiritual, moral, natural and economic crisis. It is for this reason that the Lord Jesus through his Holy Spirit is calling up Namibians to pray.

The Bible teaches us that when the nation Israel turned away from the Lord YHWH, they suffered much. They lost their freedom and were oppressed by other nations. They lost their wealth. They also suffered droughts and natural disasters. All of this was a consequence of sin.

If we look honestly at our country, we can say that all of this is true. However, "...if my people who are called by my name humble themselves, and pray and seek my face and turn from their wicked ways, then I will hear from heaven and will forgive their sin and heal their land." (2 Chronicles 7:14, ESV)

According to this scripture we need to do at least these three things.

  1. Humble ourselves
  2. Pray
  3. Seek God's face
  4. Turn from our wicked ways

We like to put blame and criticize, but "Why do you look at the speck of sawdust in your brother’s eye and pay no attention to the plank in your own eye? How can you say to your brother, ‘Brother, let me take the speck out of your eye,’ when you yourself fail to see the plank in your own eye? You hypocrite, first take the plank out of your eye, and then you will see clearly to remove the speck from your brother’s eye." (Luke 6:40-42, NIV)

As our only clear instruction at this time is to gather 1000 people to pray, our call is to our fellow Christians is this. Let us consecrate and cleanse ourselves, the same way that the Israelites did when they prepared to meet with the Lord YHWH at Mount Sinai (Ex 19:10). Let the Holy Spirit guide you in the how you should do this personally. Let each of us get ready, so that when the time comes, we can go to the mountain and meet with out God, face to face, to intercede for Namibia.
