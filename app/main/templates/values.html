Our Values and Guiding Principles

  1. Jesus Christ alone is the head of this Movement.
  2. God's Word, the Bible, will be the only manual, guide or source of guiding principles for the prayer network.
  3. We will not decide what to pray by relying on our own understanding. We will always ask the Holy Spirit for guidance.
  4. All glory belongs to God the Father, his Son Jesus and the Holy Spirit, who has chosen us for the privilege to be his representatives in Namibia and work together with him to advance his kingdom.

What does this mean in practice?

  • This prayer network has no chairperson, steering committee or any form of appointed or elected leadership. The Holy Spirit alone will lead and direct the prayer network.
  • The prayer network will not have any strategic plan that needs execution. The network will only pray in obedience to the direction of the Holy Spirit.
  • The first group of twelve (Base 12) will serve as coordinators of the prayer network. They will collect all "words", from wherever it originates in the prayer network. Once the "words" are collated, they will pray and ask the Holy Spirit for clear instruction on which "words" need to be distributed to the complete network for prayer.
